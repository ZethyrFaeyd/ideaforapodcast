Each episode of "Idea for a Podcast" starts with a completely new podcast concept. We then attempt to execute that podcast style while providing behind-the-scenes commentary about the creative process, the challenges of different formats, and the meta-narrative of podcasting itself.

From true crime parodies to cooking shows gone wrong, from meditation podcasts that can't stay focused to conspiracy theory podcasts that accidentally debunk themselves - every episode is both a new podcast AND a commentary on podcasting culture.

Latest Episodes

Episode 001

"Murder Mystery Mondays"

A true crime podcast that solves fictional crimes

We attempt to create a true crime podcast about a fictional murder mystery, complete with dramatic reenactments, suspect interviews, and a detective who's clearly making it up as he goes along.

Meta Commentary

"The challenge was maintaining the serious true crime tone while our detective kept forgetting the victim's name and accidentally solving crimes from other episodes."

45 min Listen Now
Episode 002

"Cooking with Chaos"

A cooking podcast that can't follow its own recipes

We try to host a cooking show while constantly forgetting ingredients, misreading measurements, and accidentally creating entirely new dishes through our mistakes.

Meta Commentary

"The beauty of this format is that every cooking disaster becomes a 'happy accident' and every substitution becomes 'creative cooking.'"

38 min Listen Now
Episode 003

"Mindful Moments"

A meditation podcast that can't stay focused

We attempt to create a peaceful meditation podcast while constantly getting distracted by outside noises, forgetting our breathing exercises, and accidentally starting conversations mid-meditation.

Meta Commentary

"The irony of a meditation podcast that can't maintain focus is too perfect. We're basically creating ASMR for people who can't sit still."

52 min Listen Now
Episode 004

"Conspiracy Corner"

A conspiracy theory podcast that debunks itself

We create a conspiracy theory podcast that starts with wild theories but accidentally proves them wrong through our own research and logical thinking.

Meta Commentary

"The best part is when we realize our conspiracy theory makes no sense and have to pivot to a completely different theory mid-episode."

41 min Listen Now
Episode 005

"Tech Talk Tuesday"

A tech podcast hosted by people who don't understand technology

We attempt to discuss the latest tech trends while constantly mispronouncing company names, misunderstanding basic concepts, and accidentally inventing new technologies through our confusion.

Meta Commentary

"There's something beautiful about confidently explaining technology you don't understand. We're basically creating a parody of every tech podcast ever."

47 min Listen Now
Episode 006

"Fitness Fails"

A fitness podcast by people who hate working out

We try to create motivational fitness content while constantly making excuses, finding reasons to skip workouts, and accidentally inventing new forms of 'exercise' that involve minimal movement.

Meta Commentary

"The challenge is maintaining enthusiasm for fitness while being completely honest about how much we'd rather be eating pizza and watching TV."

39 min Listen Now
